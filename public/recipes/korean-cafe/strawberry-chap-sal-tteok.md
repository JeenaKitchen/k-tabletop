## Ingredients
- 2 cups glutinous rice flour
- 1 1/4 cups warm water
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 cup fresh strawberries, diced
- 1/2 cup whipped cream
- 2 tbsp strawberry jam
- Cornstarch for dusting

## Instructions
Step 1 - Make dough: Mix glutinous rice flour, water, and sugar until smooth.
Step 2 - Steam: Steam dough for 20 minutes until translucent and cooked through.
Step 3 - Prepare filling: Mix diced strawberries with whipped cream and jam.
Step 4 - Assemble: Wrap portions of dough around filling, dust with cornstarch.
